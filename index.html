About

Myself

A logical and creative thinker who enjoys learning new languages, frameworks and technologies in general. Excited to be a part of the future technology industry. I also specialized in interface design and prototyping. I've worked designing digital experiences in a variety of contexts; at agencies and design studios, as well as with start-ups, and in-house teams.

At the Beginning of my career I have been contracting with design studios, agencies, and in-house teams. While my roles in these contracts have varied slightly in the title; from Interaction Designer, to Visual Designer, to Motion Designer — all of these roles have focused on creating design solutions from a user centered perspective in the form of digital products and services.

I spent the last few years of my career as an UX Designer on the Creative Technology team at Sri Hema Infotech in Chennai. My work there focused on the creation of websites for Vaishnave Media, Akshaya Diginet Vision, Image Network. I also made animations and pre-roll videos for various clients, and had the opportunity to work with innovative teams to create site specific installations and experiences, as well as some iOS and Android apps.
